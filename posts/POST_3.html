this ain't my home

this ain’t my home but it feels like though
the trees, welcome me with a big wave, nodding their heads
the breeze plays with my hair and teases my eyes
the river sings as i walk into my home
but it ain’t my home
the sun peeks in between the trees
trying to catch my attention
glowing its light through the leaves
turning the green into yellow
those leaves growing all over the trees
in so many colors and shades
fashion the trees in weird ways
nature dresses differently
i wish i could hug them all, for looooooong
just like my friends do :)
i wish this was my home but it ain’t but it feels like home

- 11th July 2023
